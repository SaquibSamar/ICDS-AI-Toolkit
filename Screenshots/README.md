# Dashboard Screenshots

This folder contains **screenshots of the Power BI dashboard** developed as part of the ICDS AI Toolkit.

## Purpose
The screenshots are provided to:
- Visually demonstrate how the AI insights are presented
- Show the structure and layout of the dashboard
- Help reviewers understand the solution without opening Power BI

## What the Screenshots Show
- Overall child nutrition risk distribution
- Dropout risk overview
- Child-level details with risk and dropout reasons
- Village and Anganwadi-level comparisons
- Interactive filters (Village, Gender, Risk Level)

## Note
The screenshots are based on **synthetic ICDS-like data** used for demonstration purposes only.  
The dashboard design and AI logic remain fully compatible with authorised real ICDS datasets.
